# Profile Photo Setup

## Required File

Place your professional headshot at:
```
public/profile-photo.jpg
```

## Recommended Specifications

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions:** At least 800x800 pixels (square aspect ratio)
- **File size:** Under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Subject:** Professional headshot with good lighting
- **Background:** Solid color or minimal, non-distracting background

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Composition:** Center your face in the frame — the image will be cropped to a circle
2. **Lighting:** Use even, front-facing light to avoid harsh shadows
3. **Expression:** Friendly, approachable, professional
4. **Clothing:** Business casual or professional attire
5. **Background:** Clean, solid color works best (the circle crop will hide the edges)

The Hero section will automatically display this image in a circular frame with an accent border.
